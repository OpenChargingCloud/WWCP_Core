Filter Expression Help

Basics
ProdMatch lines containing "Prod" (case-insensitive substring)
"charging started"Quoted substring — useful if the term contains special characters
/E\\\\d{6}/Regular expression match
/charging/iRegex with flags (i = case-insensitive)
Operators
A & BAND — both A and B must match
A | BOR — either A or B must match
!ANOT — exclude lines matching A
( … )Grouping — override default precedence
Precedence
! (NOT) binds tightest, then & (AND), then | (OR).
A | B & Cis parsed as A | (B & C)
Examples
Prod & chargingLines that contain both "Prod" and "charging"
Prod | QALines with "Prod" or "QA"
Prod & !chargingLines with "Prod" but without "charging"
(Prod | QA) & charging"Prod" or "QA", and also "charging"
Prod & /E\\\\d{3,}/"Prod" and an EVSE-id-like pattern
